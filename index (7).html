What we do

Three problems we solve.

Most small businesses have data scattered everywhere and no clear picture. We fix that — quickly, affordably, in plain French or English.

📊

Dashboards & Reporting

We build clear, live dashboards that show you what's actually happening in your business — sales, inventory, ops — without you needing to touch a spreadsheet.

⚙️

Process Automation

Repetitive tasks cost you hours every week. We identify and automate them — from data entry to reports to notifications — so your team can focus on what matters.

Data Strategy & CRM

We help you connect your tools — CRM, spreadsheets, logistics systems — into a single source of truth. Better data in, better decisions out.
